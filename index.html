My name is Bonnie

and I'm a recently qualified yoga teacher looking for students. My practice is based heavily around alignment and breathing - with a strong emphasis on fundamentals and building mobility and ease in everyday movement.

I'm here to create personalized practices that draw from Hatha, Vinyasa, Pranayama, and meditation to build strength and find balance. These sessions should help you build a strong foundation for your home or studio practice, or keep you accountable with personalised attention.
